About

Sirikata is a BSD-licensed open source platform for virtual worlds.

Open3dHub is the content distribution website for the Sirikata platform. COLLADA documents can be uploaded by any user and then get processed in the background for use in the Sirikata virtual world.

Open3dHub is being developed primarily by Jeff Terrace, as part of the Scalable Network Systems Group at Princeton University

The work is funded by the National Science Foundation (NeTS-ANET Grants #0831163 and #0831374) and by the Intel Science and Technology Center for Visual Computing. The positions or opinions of this project do not necessarily represent the official policy of any of these organizations.
